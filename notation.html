Notation¶

Pymicra uses a specific notation to name each one of its columns. This notation is extremely important, because it is by these labels that Pymicra knows which variable is in each column, so we advise at the very least a quick look at this chapter to understand the basics of naming.

The notation can be checked by opening the IPython terminal (because of its autocomplete function) and entering

begin{terminal} begin{alltt} import pymicra dir(pymicra.notation) end{alltt} end{terminal}
